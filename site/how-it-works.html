The process

From handover pack to digital home record.

Home Compass is designed to make property handover simpler for developers and more useful for homeowners.

Request a Pilot Join the Waitlist
app.homecompass.co / handover
1

Create the property profile

A home profile is created for each property with details like address, plot number, development name, property type, and handover status.

2

Add handover records

The developer or admin team uploads relevant home documents, warranties, certificates, manuals, guides, contacts, and support information.

3

Invite the homeowner

The homeowner receives access to their My Home dashboard, where information is presented clearly and simply.

4

Use after move-in

The homeowner can continue using the record to check documents, track updates, log repairs, and manage reminders.

5

Keep the home record alive

As the home changes over time, the record can continue to grow with repairs, maintenance, ownership updates, and future documents.

Two views, one record

Developers manage the estate. Homeowners see their home.

The same property record powers both experiences: an operations view for developer and estate teams, and a simple My Home dashboard for the people who move in.
