The gap

After completion, buyers still need support.

The sales journey may be polished, but the handover and aftercare experience often depends on scattered documents, manual follow-ups, and repeated questions.

Homeowners need clarity. Aftercare teams need structure. Developers need a better way to manage the information that comes with every home.

What you gain

Five ways Home Compass earns its place.

1

Better buyer experience

Give homeowners a clear and modern way to access their home information after move-in.

2

Fewer repeated questions

Make documents, warranties, manuals, and key contacts easier to find.

3

Consistent handover process

Create a repeatable digital structure for every home, plot, or development phase.

4

Stronger brand perception

A clean digital handover experience makes the developer feel more organised, modern, and buyer-focused.

5

Easier internal coordination

Sales, handover, customer care, and aftercare teams can work from a clearer property record.

In the platform

Handover readiness, plot by plot.

Track document status, handover dates, defects, and readiness for every plot in a development. Bulk-upload handover packs and let Home Compass match files to the right plots and document types.

Missing documents, recent activity, and aftercare issues stay visible in one place, so nothing slips between completion and move-in.
