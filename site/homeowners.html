For homeowners

Your home should come with a memory.

Home Compass gives homeowners one organised place for documents, warranties, manuals, repairs, reminders, and key information about their home.

Join the Waitlist
app.homecompass.co / my-home
The problem

Important home information gets lost too easily.

After moving in, homeowners often have to search through emails, paper packs, PDFs, drawers, and old messages to find important information.

Home Compass makes the home easier to manage by keeping everything in one simple digital record.

What you gain

Five things your home record does for you.

1

Find documents faster

Access key home records without searching through folders or inboxes.

2

Know what is covered

Store warranties, certificates, guarantees, and coverage details in one place.

3

Keep repair history

Track repairs, updates, and maintenance work over time.

4

Set useful reminders

Stay on top of servicing, warranty dates, safety checks, and maintenance tasks.

5

Be ready for resale or rental

Keep a cleaner record of the property for future sale, rental, or ownership changes.

In the platform

When something breaks, everything you need is already there.

Log an issue, and Home Compass links it to the right appliance, its manual, and its warranty automatically. A full issue timeline keeps track of what happened and when.

Need a tradesperson? Share a secure link with only the details they need — the issue, photos, the manual, and the warranty — without handing over your whole home record.
